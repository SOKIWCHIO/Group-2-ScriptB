Active and Transfer Learning with Partially Bayesian Neural Networks for Materials and Chemicals by Maxim Ziatdinov

𝐃𝐞𝐬𝐜𝐫𝐢𝐩𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧: We demonstrate that Partially Bayesian Neural Networks (PBNNs), which incorporate probabilistic weights selectively within layers, yield accuracy and uncertainty estimations comparable to Fully Bayesian Networks on active learning tasks across molecular and materials science, while significantly reducing computational expense. Additionally, we show how initializing prior distributions with weights pre-trained in theoretical ("digital twin") space enables PBNNs to effectively leverage computational predictions to accelerate active learning in experiments.
