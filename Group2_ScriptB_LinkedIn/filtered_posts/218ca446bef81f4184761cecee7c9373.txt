NEW on nanoHUB: Uncertainty in Materials Science Property Prediction: The Good, The Bad, and The Uncalibrated

This presentation by Ashley Dale of the University of Toronto, introduces the concepts of aleatoric and epistemic uncertainty within #machinelearning and demonstrates how to use ALIGNN models for predicting bandgap values from a dataset to introduce an uncertainty characterization workflow.  

A Jupyter notebook acts as scaffolded coding exercise to enable participants in improving coding skills while developing an intuition about the trustworthiness of model predictions:
