Curious how AI models can know when they don’t know—especially in critical fields like ophthalmology?
I just published a technical breakdown of FMUE, a new transformer-based foundation model for OCT diagnosis that doesn’t just output predictions, but also quantifies its own uncertainty. With an evidential deep learning head, FMUE can flag unfamiliar or ambiguous cases for expert review—making it a step closer to trustworthy, risk-aware clinical AI.
In the article, I explore:
- Why uncertainty quantification is essential for safe medical AI
- How the model uses Dirichlet-based outputs for interpretable confidence
- The underlying architecture (ViT-Large + LoRA fine-tuning)
- How it compares to clinicians and previous models
- What this means for real-world deployment in healthcare
Check out the deep-dive here below: https://lnkd.in/eWEkA3pr #AI #medtech #foundationalmodel
