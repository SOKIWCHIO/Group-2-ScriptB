- Addressing Missing Modalities: SURE enhances pretrained multimodal models by reconstructing missing modalities while providing reliable uncertainty estimates.
- Innovative Loss Function: The introduction of a Pearson Correlation-based loss and statistical error propagation techniques allows for precise quantification of uncertainties from missing data and model predictions.
- Robust Performance Across Tasks: Extensive experiments demonstrate that SURE achieves state-of-the-art performance in sentiment analysis, genre classification, and action recognition.

This framework not only improves interpretability but also enhances overall model performance even when faced with incomplete data.
