I’m excited to share that our new research paper titled 'Uncertainty-Informed Volume Visualization using Implicit Neural Representation' has been accepted for publication in the IEEE Workshop on Uncertainty Visualization: Applications, Techniques, Software, and Decision Frameworks in conjunction with IEEE Visualization Conference 2024, Florida, USA! This work delves into uncertainty quantification and its visual interpretation for volume rendering results when compressive deep learning models are used to reconstruct volume datasets. We study different methods of uncertainty estimation techniques for deep learning models and how such prediction (epistemic) uncertainty helps in better interpreting the volume rendering results, making the predictions more explainable and trustworthy.

Special thanks to my students Shanu Saklani, Chitwan Goel, Shrey Bansal  for their dedication and hard work. I also thank my collaborators from Oak Ridge National Lab. and University of Utah for their contributions!

A preprint is available at: https://lnkd.in/gQ75HngZ
