🎉 Happy to announce the publication of our latest research in IEEE Access! 🎉

Our paper, "Adversarially Robust Fault Zone Prediction in Smart Grids With Bayesian Neural Networks", tackles two major challenges in smart grid fault prediction: adversarial vulnerabilities and false alarms.

Using Bayesian Neural Networks (BNNs), we achieved high level of accuracy and developed an uncertainty-based detection scheme that successfully distinguishes between normal and adversarial data. This framework offers a promising approach to bolstering smart grid security while ensuring stability and efficiency as energy demands grow.

🔗 Check out the full paper here: https://lnkd.in/e-TGkmra

A special thanks to my co-authors Emad Efatinasab, Alberto Sinigaglia, Nahal Azadi, and Mirco Rampazzo for their collaboration and hard work. 🙌

Proud to contribute to this exciting research as part of the AMCO Lab - UniPD 🚀
