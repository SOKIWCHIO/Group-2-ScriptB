If you are interested in uncertainty quantification, check out my new video on Gaussian Process (GP) regression. Gaussian Processes define a distribution over functions for which the posterior can be computed in closed form
once we observe some data.

Applications of GP regression include:
1. Bayesian Optimization: hyperparameter tuning, experimental design
2. Time Series Forecasting: financial markets
3. Spatial Data Analysis:  geo-statistics, weather prediction
4. Robotics: trajectory planning, RL
and more
