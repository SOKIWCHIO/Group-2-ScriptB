New pre-print on conformal credal sets – a collaboration led by Alireza Javanmardi and with Eyke Hüllermeier: https://lnkd.in/ebxRpVVU

In a nutshell, we assume first-order data where we have multiple annotations per example that allows us to get an approximate distribution over labels (for multiclass classification). Then, we directly conformalize these categorical distributions, constructing credal sets (sets of distributions) with an associated coverage guarantee. The corresponding credal sets intuitively represent both epistemic and aleatoric uncertainty, as shown on ChaosNLI.
