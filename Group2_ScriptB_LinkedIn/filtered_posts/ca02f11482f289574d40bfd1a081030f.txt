🎉 Exciting news! My latest research paper, "Subgraph Isomorphic Decision Tree to Predict Radical Thermochemistry with Bounded Uncertainty Estimation," has just been published in the Journal of Physical Chemistry A!

We compiled a dataset of thermochemical parameters for 2210 radicals derived from quantum chemical calculations. We extended the subgraph isomorphic decision tree (SIDT) machine learning algorithm to estimate hydrogen bond increment corrections. The SIDT estimator marks a leap in kinetic modeling, providing a more interpretable, reliable, and scalable framework for predicting radical thermochemistry.

I'm deeply grateful for the collaboration and insights from my co-authors, Xiaorui Dong, Matt Johnson, and William Green. For those interested in diving deeper into our findings and methodologies, you can read the full publication here:

Subgraph Isomorphic Decision Tree to Predict Radical Thermochemistry with Bounded Uncertainty Estimation
