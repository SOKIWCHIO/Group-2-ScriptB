Real-world numerical data is often corrupted by data entry/processing mistakes, sensor noise, imperfect human estimates, ...

Our newest research paper “Detecting Errors in Numerical Data via any Regression Model” presents a general algorithm to automatically detect such corrupted values.  For example this can be used by: businesses to handle incorrect amounts in invoices sent/paid & price listings, or scientists to handle incorrect counts of cells in a culture.

Able to detect errors with higher precision/recall than RANSAC and conformal inference, our method accounts for aleatoric/epistemic uncertainties from a model trained to predict these values from the available information in a dataset.  We've open-sourced code for this method in cleanlab for you to easily catch errors in your own datasets. 

Blog post to learn more:  https://lnkd.in/gs2fJZzr
