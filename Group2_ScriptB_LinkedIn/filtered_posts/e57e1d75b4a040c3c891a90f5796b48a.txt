What a wonderful week in Banff! I'm here at the Banff International Research Station for the Workshop on Uncertainty Quantification in Neural Network Models.  This small intensive workshop has been an amazing opportunity to learn from computer scientists, mathematicians, and engineers working on #neuralnetworks of every variety from PINNs to GPT. The talks are long (each speaker has 1-1.5 hours) so we can dive deeper and have meaningful conversations about UQ and ML. 

I am especially grateful to the organizers Habib Najm, Roger Ghanem, yue yu, Serge Prudhomme, and Prasanna Balaprakash for inviting me to give a presentation on "Recent Developments and Future Opportunities in Bayesian Neural Networks." Thanks to the engaging discussion, I learned even more about my own work and its place in the larger landscape beyond engineering.
