Looking for a computationally effective and reliable uncertainty estimation method for your segmentation problem? ⏲ 📈 My postdoc Kaisar Kushibar developed Layer Ensembles, a method that uses a single network and requires only a single pass to estimate epistemic uncertainty of a network, saving training and prediction time while still giving you competitive results.

🖥 Easy to wrap your existing DL model, the instructions are availabile in this github repo: https://lnkd.in/d6Aiu3gY
