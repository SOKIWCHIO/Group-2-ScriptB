A Practical Approach to Bayesian Deep Learning" is currently presented at #AAAI2025.

The computational complexity of Bayesian learning has long been a barrier to its practical adoption in large-scale tasks. While Bayesian Neural Networks (BNNs) offer exceptional robustness and resilience to unseen or out-of-distribution inputs, their high computational cost often hinders their practicality. With Bella, we introduce a novel framework that dramatically reduces the computational burden of BNNs. By applying multiple low-rank perturbations to parameters from a pre-trained neural network, Bella allows both vanilla deep ensembles and advanced techniques like Stein Variational Gradient Descent (SVGD) to scale effectivelyâ€”even for large models.
