🌟 Unlock the Power of #Economic #Data with #LLMs! 🌟

Join us on Friday, November 22 at 11 AM ET / 5 PM CET for an exciting #AMLEDS #Webinar with Sergio Correia from the Federal Reserve Board, who will present "Unlocking Economic Data with Large Language Models: An Update."

Moderated by Sophia Kazinnik from the Federal Reserve Bank of Richmond, this session will explore how Large Language Models (LLMs) are revolutionizing the way we unlock and analyze economic data.

🔑 What you’ll learn:

How using LLMs is accessible to everyone—just a few lines of Python code required.
Practical applications, such as summarizing text, extracting historical data, merging datasets, and leveraging embeddings for tasks like text classification and regression.
A deep dive into Retrieval-Augmented Generation (#RAG) pipelines for generating data with LLMs.

If you’re curious about how LLMs can unlock new possibilities in economic data analysis, don’t miss this webinar!
