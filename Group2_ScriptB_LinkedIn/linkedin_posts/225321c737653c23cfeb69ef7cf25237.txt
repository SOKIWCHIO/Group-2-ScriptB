The Deanship of Applied Research and Entrepreneurship (DARE) at Bahrain Polytechnic organized a "Research Talk Series” session on “Research Orientation: Leveraging AI for modern Scientific research and research dissemination”. The session was facilitated by Dr. Khalid Ouanes, Assistant Professor position at the Health Informatics Department of the Saudi Electronic University. Dr. Khalid introduced his audience to the components of AI and their application in scientific research, and elaborated on the use of AI in research dissemination
