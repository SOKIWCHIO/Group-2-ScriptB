Happy to share our latest publication in the 𝐉𝐨𝐮𝐫𝐧𝐚𝐥 𝐨𝐟 𝐄𝐚𝐫𝐭𝐡𝐪𝐮𝐚𝐤𝐞 𝐄𝐧𝐠𝐢𝐧𝐞𝐞𝐫𝐢𝐧𝐠, led by Dr. Jyothi Yedulla and co-authored by me, Anuj Choubey, Kammula Sai Tulasi, and Prof. Raghukanth STG! 

📄 Title: Bayesian Neural Network-Based Ground Motion Model for Horizontal and V/H Spectral Ordinates with Epistemic Uncertainty for the European Region

Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) have revolutionized the development of non-parametric Ground Motion Models, but they often overlook epistemic uncertainty, providing only deterministic-point estimates. In this study, we have explored the power of Bayesian Neural Networks (BNNs)—probabilistic models that can predict median, aleatory, as well as epistemic uncertainty. 

📊 Why does it matter?
Better uncertainty modeling for seismic hazard analysis 🔍
Improved reliability in ground motion predictions for performance-based design 🏗️
A step toward probabilistic, data-driven earthquake engineering 🧩
