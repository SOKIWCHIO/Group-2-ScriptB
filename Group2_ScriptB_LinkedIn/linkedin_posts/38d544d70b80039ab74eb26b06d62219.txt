This work delves into uncertainty quantification and its visual interpretation for volume rendering results when compressive deep learning models are used to reconstruct volume datasets. We study different methods of uncertainty estimation techniques for deep learning models and how such prediction (epistemic) uncertainty helps in better interpreting the volume rendering results, making the predictions more explainable and trustworthy.
