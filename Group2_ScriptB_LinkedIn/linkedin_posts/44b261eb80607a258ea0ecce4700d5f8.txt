✨ Excited to share my internship project with NVIDIA: 𝐔𝐧𝐜𝐞𝐫𝐭𝐚𝐢𝐧𝐭𝐲 𝐄𝐬𝐭𝐢𝐦𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 𝐟𝐨𝐫 3𝐃 𝐎𝐛𝐣𝐞𝐜𝐭 𝐃𝐞𝐭𝐞𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 𝐯𝐢𝐚 𝐄𝐯𝐢𝐝𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐢𝐚𝐥 𝐋𝐞𝐚𝐫𝐧𝐢𝐧𝐠! ✨

During my internship, we developed a 𝐧𝐨𝐯𝐞𝐥, 𝐟𝐚𝐬𝐭, and 𝐬𝐜𝐚𝐥𝐚𝐛𝐥𝐞 framework for 𝐮𝐧𝐜𝐞𝐫𝐭𝐚𝐢𝐧𝐭𝐲 𝐞𝐬𝐭𝐢𝐦𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 in 3𝐃 𝐨𝐛𝐣𝐞𝐜𝐭 𝐝𝐞𝐭𝐞𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧, leveraging Evidential Learning to improve reliability and robustness in critical applications like autonomous vehicles and robotics. This solution seamlessly integrates uncertainty into Bird's Eye View (BEV) representations, addressing key challenges such as:

∘ Detecting out-of-distribution (OOD) scenes
∘ Identifying erroneous bounding boxes
∘ Highlighting missed detections

Importantly, our method is 𝐜𝐨𝐦𝐩𝐮𝐭𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧𝐚𝐥𝐥𝐲 𝐞𝐟𝐟𝐢𝐜𝐢𝐞𝐧𝐭 and 𝐝𝐨𝐞𝐬 𝐧𝐨𝐭 𝐫𝐞𝐪𝐮𝐢𝐫𝐞 𝐜𝐨𝐦𝐩𝐥𝐢𝐜𝐚𝐭𝐞𝐝 𝐫𝐞𝐭𝐫𝐚𝐢𝐧𝐢𝐧𝐠, making it ideal for real-world deployment.

✨ Key Achievements:
∘ Up to 20% improvement over baselines in detecting OOD scenes and localization errors.
∘ Integration into a fully uncertainty-driven auto-labeling framework, enhancing detection metrics while remaining computationally efficient.
