Understanding what an AI system "does not know" is an important step towards developing truly trustworthy and explainable AI. For black box AI systems built using Deep Learning, principled uncertainty quantification is a powerful method to determine when to trust the output of such systems. Following the release of Bayesian-Torch, a framework allowing developers to seamlessly convert target DNN models to equivalent Bayesian Neural Networks with Uncertainty Quantification, we are glad to announce the availability of comprehensive quantization capabilities as part of the Bayesian-Torch. Now users can build uncertainty aware AI systems without sacrificing the inference speed. Hit our blog for more details. Ranganath Krishnan, Keyur Ranipa, Mahesh Subedar
