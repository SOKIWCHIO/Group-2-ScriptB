Very interesting presentation! Nice how you show that the well tuned pipeline containing a LogisticRegression model results in well calibrated model predictions. I actually don't think this would work with all classification algorithms. For example, a RandomForestClassifier will almost never result in well calibrated probabilities, even after applying thourough hyperparameter tuning. The algorithm is unlikely to produce probabilities close to 0 and 1, because for these scores all trees in the ensemble need to predict 0 or 1 in order for the mean to be 0 or 1.
