🚀 Exciting times at DeepLearn 2024! 🚀

Professor: Sergei V. Gleyzer
University: The University of Alabama
Course: Machine Learning Fundamentals and Their Applications to Very Large Scientific Data: Rare Signal and Feature Extraction, End-to-End Deep Learning, Uncertainty Estimation and Realtime Machine Learning Applications in Software and Hardware
Level: Introductory/Intermediate
Dates: 15th - 19th of July
Location: Universidade da Maia - UMAIA

📅 Course Highlights:

Machine Learning Basics: Get introduced to the fundamentals of machine learning and classification theory based on statistical learning.

Popular Algorithms: Delve into two classes of popular algorithms: decision and rule-based methods (decision trees, bagging and boosting random forests) and deep neural network-based models (fully-connected, convolutional, recurrent/LSTM, graph neural networks, transformers).

Practical Applications: Focus on practical applications in analyzing large scientific data, ensuring interpretability, and estimating uncertainty.

Feature Extraction: Learn how to best extract meaningful features from large datasets.

Realtime Deep Learning: Explore the implementation of real-time deep learning algorithms in both software and hardware.

No Prior Experience Needed: This course is designed for beginners; no previous machine learning background is required.

Join us to gain a comprehensive introduction to machine learning and its applications in analyzing very large scientific data, and enhance your skills in this rapidly evolving field.
