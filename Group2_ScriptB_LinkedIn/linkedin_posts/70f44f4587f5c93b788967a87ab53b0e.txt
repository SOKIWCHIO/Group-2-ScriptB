Javier Antorán的动态
Javier Antorán

Co-founder and CEO at Angstrom AI

3 年

For the past year, I have been working on the linearised Laplace method for probabilistic inference with neural networks. This method allows us to choose hyperparameters without costly cross-validation and provides very well-calibrated uncertainty estimates to accompany neural network predictions. However, I found that a naive application of lin. Laplace to modern deep learning systems can fail dramatically.

My new paper: "Adapting the Linearised Laplace Model Evidence for Modern Deep Learning" identifies pathologies in the lin. Laplace method when applied to modern NNs and provides an adapted methodology that fixes these issues!

Our proposals are derived for first principles and then empirically validated on Transformers, LeNet-style CNNs, ResNets with batchnorm and with (non-scale-invariant) fixup, Pre-ResNets, and U-net autoencoders.

I will be presenting the work at ICML2022 in Baltimore, USA.
The paper can be found at https://lnkd.in/ePyUDDbZ
