AI for drug discovery

6 个月

Our research on ML uncertainty quantification (UQ) was accepted at AISTATS 2025! In this paper, we developed and validated a new metric for UQ. 

I posted before about the importance of  ML robustness, especially when methods are deployed in real world biomedical settings. As ML systems become more prevalent in healthcare applications, understanding when our models are uncertain is essential for safe deployment. However, uncertainty estimation is complex and difficult to validate. By providing theoretically grounded metrics to evaluate the utility of uncertainty estimator, our findings pave the way for broader adoption of UQ in deep learning applications. 

Read the preprint here: https://lnkd.in/eeZA-QnP

Congrats to our talented team Arthur Pignet, Chiara Régniez and John Klein
