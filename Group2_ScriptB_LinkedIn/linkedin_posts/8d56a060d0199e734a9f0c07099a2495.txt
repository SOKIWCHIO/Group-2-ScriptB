🚀 Turning Messy Data into Insight

In STEM, data is rarely clean. 📊 We deal with scattered points, noisy curves, and confusing patterns. The challenge is: how do we transform all that into knowledge we can use?

That’s exactly what my new book, Data-driven Modeling in Python, is all about. 🐍

Inside, you’ll find practical tools and examples, including:
✔️ Linear & polynomial regression for trend discovery
✔️ Gaussian Process Regression for uncertainty-aware predictions
✔️ Random Forests, Neural Nets, and other ML models
✔️ Feature engineering, dimensionality reduction, & selection
✔️ Symbolic regression to uncover interpretable equations
✔️ Scikit-learn pipelines, cross-validation, & custom estimators

This resource is written for students, researchers, and professionals who want to move from raw data → to clear insights → to predictive models.

📘 Check it out here: https://lnkd.in/ehj8T5Tt

If you’re looking to strengthen your data science toolkit in a scientific or engineering context, this book was written with you in mind.
