Machine learning, Deep Learning and Computer Vision @ FAU

Our recent research in #machinelearning and systems is also the motivation behind our teaching activities. At FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg, we are currently building our new lab course that will teach our students how to bring machine learning algorithms to resource-constrained agents, including topics such as uncertainty estimation, neural architecture design, and domain adaptation.
