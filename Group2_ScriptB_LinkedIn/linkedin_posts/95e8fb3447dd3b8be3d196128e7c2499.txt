Imagine a world where all of your neural network hyperparameters are set automatically to the values that generalise best, without the need for held-out cross-validation data! 

You can keep using a held-out set to tune hyperparameters or you can take the Bayesian pill and see how deep the rabbit hole goes. Watch my talk on model selection by adapting the linearised Laplace model evidence for modern neural networks at the symposium for advances in approximate Bayesian inference tomorrow Feb 1st at 18:40 GMT (https://lnkd.in/eKBhAhux).

The full paper, titled "Linearised Laplace Inference in Networks with Normalisation Layers and the Neural g-Prior" is available at https://lnkd.in/eEJ2Q_Bs.
