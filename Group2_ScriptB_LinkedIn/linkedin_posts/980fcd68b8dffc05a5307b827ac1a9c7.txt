Very happy to announce that the latest publication I was collaborating on is now published in the Journal of Chemical Theory and Computation. What started as a semester thesis under the supervision of Stephan Thaler and Julija Zavadlav, turned into a very interesting publication, analyzing methods for scalable Bayesian uncertainty quantification for neural network potentials. 

I am looking forward to future work in the field of neural network modelling and uncertainty quantification 😀
