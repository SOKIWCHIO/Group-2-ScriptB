🚀 Is Probability the Future of AI?
 As AI moves beyond labels and boundaries, one thing is clear — probabilistic models are rising fast.
Unlike traditional supervised or unsupervised methods, density estimation doesn't just predict...
 It understands, generates, and imagines.
From ChatGPT to generative video to autonomous systems, the future lies in models that:
 ✅ Handle uncertainty
 ✅ Learn from fewer labels
 ✅ Generate new, intelligent outputs
We’re entering a world where "What’s the probability?" becomes more powerful than "What’s the label?".
📌 My takeaway:
Probabilistic modeling isn't just another tool — it's the foundation of future-ready AI.
