This semester, my Iowa State University - Ivy College of Business class will explore and flesh out a process of leveraging a combination of Design Thinking plus Thinking Like a Data Scientist concepts, templates, and canvases to create a map that transforms conflict and tension into genuine innovation.

Here is a video of me explaining what I hope to achieve over the next 10+ weeks, and I know that my aspiring students are up for joining me on this exercise.

This exercise is critical - the ability to leverage the diverse and sometimes competing perspectives and rationales from a diverse set of stakeholders and constituents, if we have any hope of developing AI models that deliver more meaningful, relevant, responsible, and ethical outcomes.

I know where we are starting, and I know what the endpoint looks like.  But I'm not sure of the journey to get there.  And that is what makes teaching so much fun!

BTW, many thanks to John Morley, who put me on this path many years ago.  Well, John, it is finally starting to sink in!
