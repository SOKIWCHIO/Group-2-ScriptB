🌍 Honored to present our work "Meta-UNet: Enhancing Skin-Lesion Segmentation with Multimodal Feature Integration and Uncertainty Estimation" at the CARS 2025 (Computer Assisted Radiology and Surgery) conference in Berlin, Germany! 

In this study, we enhanced the U-Net architecture by integrating lesion-specific metadata, enabling the model to deliver more confident and interpretable segmentation results. We explored how metadata fusion helps the model not only perform better but also express its certainty in predictions — a crucial step toward safe and trustworthy AI in healthcare. 🚀
Grateful to my amazing co-authors Alaysia LeilaniStone and Miguel Angel Gonzalez Ballester for their invaluable guidance and collaboration!
