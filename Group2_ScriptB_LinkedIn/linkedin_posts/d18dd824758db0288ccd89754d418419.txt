I am excited to share that my latest research paper, titled “Artificial Neural Network Model for Concrete Strength Predictions Based on Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity Measurement,” has been published in the Materials Journal.
In this paper, we propose two mathematical models using nonlinear regression and artificial neural networks (ANNs) to accurately predict the compressive strength of concrete based on ultrasonic pulse velocity (UPV) measurements. 
Our models have demonstrated exceptional accuracy and reliability, with high coefficients of determination and low standard errors.
