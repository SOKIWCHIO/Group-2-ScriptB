#UBuffalo’s Haimonti Dutta and Anushka Tiwari presented their paper, Content-Based Art Recommendation Using Multimodal Graph Neural Networks, at the 15th IEEE International Conference on Knowledge Graphs (ICKG2024) Conference held in Abu Dhabi, UAE in December. 

Haimonti Dutta is Associate Professor, Management Science and Systems, in the University at Buffalo School of Management, The State University of New York, and an affiliated faculty member of the Institute for AI and Data Science (IAD). She is advisor to Anushka Tiwari, a PhD student in Computational and Data-Enabled Sciences.

The annual IEEE International Conference on Knowledge Graph (ICKG) provides an international forum for presentation of original research results in knowledge discovery and graph learning, discussion of opportunities and challenges, as well as exchange and dissemination of innovative, practical development experiences. The conference covers all aspects of knowledge discovery from data, with a strong focus on graph learning and knowledge graph, including algorithms, software, platforms.
