We are one step closer to enhancing antimicrobial treatment with artificial intelligence and machine learning! We published our paper where we developed a machine learning model to predict antibiotic plasma concentrations and compared it to a currently used method, called Population Pharmacokinetics. This model even provides uncertainty quantification to the predictions to offer even more interpretability and assistance for clinicians and is evaluated using a new uncertainty quantification metric. Go have a look if you are interested! #machinelearning
