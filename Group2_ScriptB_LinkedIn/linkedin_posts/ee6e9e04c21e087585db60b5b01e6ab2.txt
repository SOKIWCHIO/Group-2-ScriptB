I believe I am the first one to introduce INR to seismic inversion including FWI (I prefer to call it implicit FWI or implicit seismic inversion). Beyond that, we have proved that such "implicit strategy" can also be extended to eletromagnetic and GPR inversion. There are several benefits of performing inversion with INR, the convenient and no-extra-time-cost uncertainty analysis is one of them.

Same idea can also be done in the uncertainty analysis of FWI's results,  see our work in 2023 (Multilayer Perceptron and Bayesian Neural Network-Based Elastic Implicit Full Waveform Inversion, https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10097732).
