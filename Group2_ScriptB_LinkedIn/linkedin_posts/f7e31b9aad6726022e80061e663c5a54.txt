UQ in PINNS and Neural Operators: This is a critical topic for trustworthy AI in Scientific Machine Learning, but very few works have focused on this, especially for noisy inputs, not just noisy outputs to the network. In this new study https://lnkd.in/esNBZPkD, Zongren Zou and Xuhui Meng develop a new Bayesian framework for UQ for noisy inputs-outputs, and aply it to PINNS, DeepOnet and FNO.
